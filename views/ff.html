Fort Funston Trail

  • Distance: 2 miles
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Route type: Loop
<<<<<<< HEAD =======

Fort Funston features beaufiful wild flowers, 200 foot high sandy bluffs on San Francisco's SW coast where the winds blow reliably wildly. A network of trails make it ideal for hiking and horseback riding, and is good for all skill levels. This trail is moderately trafficed, accessible year-round and dog owners will be happy to know they can take leashes off here.

Popular uses: hiking, walking, nature trips, birdwatching.
